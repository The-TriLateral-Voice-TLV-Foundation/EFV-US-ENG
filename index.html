What is TLV?

The TriLateral Voice (TLV) is a comprehensive communication framework that honors the full dimensionality of human experience by attending to three integrated voices:

  • Speaker's Voice — What you say (the technical content)
  • Emotional/Somatic Voice — How it feels (the embodied experience)
  • Observer's Voice — Why it matters (the contextual significance and witness)
